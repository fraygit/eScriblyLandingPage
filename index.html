Cloud Maternity Management Software

Maternity Care just got better, organized and faster with eScribly

Get involved and sign-up for BETA!

As a cloud based software, eScribly is focused on delivering excellent services to the midwives and other lead maternty carers at large. It has been designed with quality first, and predictability. Besides that, eScribly is an innovative software that allows for an end-to-end recording of all the information patterning the maternity care of your clients. It encompasses organized administration, and clinical data recording such as pre-natal visits, birth plan, vital statistics, and postpartum visits. The best part is, its enables patients to interact directly with maternity carers, developing trust as well as delivering outstanding care through the use of generic or specific interfaces and electronic fillable forms.

High-end Security

Hosted on one of the most secured cloud platform that is duly accepted by New Zealand Ministry of Heealth. This makes sure that your data and clients information is locked from third parties in an appropriate manner.

Intuitive Patient Portal

With an intuitive portal and fillable forms, maternity management has become easier for clients and care givers to capture and record data with ease. Its self-explanatory dashboard improves the response rate of clients to routine checkups.

Fast Startup

It’s fast, and readily available to be used. This will facilitate the time needed to capture or retrieve your client’s data when needed.

Available Across Different Platforms

Its availability across numerous platforms such as mobile devices, android, iOS and Microsoft , improves customers’ interaction and usage, and as such improves the ROI of any maternity management agency.

Cloud Data Backup

This is one of the major benefits that comes with the eScribly maternity management software. It regularly backs-up data and makes it retrievable in case there is an unexpected system break down. This ensures that your patient data will always be available.
